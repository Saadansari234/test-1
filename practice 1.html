Trendy Dorm Room Decor Ideas That Turn It Into A Second Home.


Living in a dorm room is one of the coolest ways to enjoy your college campus culture! Not to mention, it allows you to make a lot of new friends and create memories as you bond with them. While dorms are shared spaces with a couple of people living in the same room, it doesn’t mean you can’t create a cosy and inviting corner in it!

Your dorm room’s overall look directly affects how you feel and function personally, socially and academically. So it’s important to spruce it up as per your unique style. Think of it as a blank canvas and start painting it with your ideas! But first, here’s a breakdown of what sets a dormitory apart from other accommodation forms.

How are dorm rooms different from apartments?

  1. A large space without any partitions accommodating several students
  2. Bunk beds to save on the already limited and small floor space
